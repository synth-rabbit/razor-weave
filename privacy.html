Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 19, 2025

Overview

Razorweave ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you visit our website.

Information We Collect

Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • Browser Information: Browser type, version, and settings
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent, referring websites
  • Local Storage: We use browser localStorage to save your dark mode preference

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do NOT require user accounts or registration
  • We do NOT collect personal information (name, email, address)
  • We do NOT use cookies for tracking
  • We do NOT use analytics services (Google Analytics, etc.)
  • We do NOT collect payment information

How We Use Information

The minimal technical information automatically collected by your browser is used only to:

  • Serve web pages correctly
  • Remember your dark mode preference (stored locally in your browser only)
  • Ensure website functionality

We do NOT sell, rent, or share any information with third parties.

Third-Party Services

Our website uses the following third-party services:

  • Google Fonts: For typography. See Google's Privacy Policy
  • GitHub Pages: For hosting. See GitHub's Privacy Policy

These services may collect technical information according to their own privacy policies.

External Links

Our website may contain links to external sites (itch.io, DriveThruRPG, etc.). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these sites. Please review their privacy policies separately.

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us.

Data Security

Since we collect minimal data and do not store personal information, there is minimal data to secure. Your dark mode preference is stored only in your browser's local storage and never transmitted to us.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access any information we may have (though we collect minimal data)
  • Request deletion of data (clear your browser's localStorage)
  • Opt out of any tracking (none exists beyond standard web server logs)
  • Use the website without providing personal information

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of changes by posting the new policy on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or legal concerns, please use our Legal Contact Form.

For general questions or support, please visit our GitHub repository issues page.

# Condition Effects Quick Lookup

Quick reference for all common conditions and their effects.

---

## Physical Conditions

### Wounded
**Cause**: Damage, injury, failed saves
**Effect**: Disadvantage on physical actions (Athletics, Combat, etc.)
**Recovery**: Short rest (partial), Long rest (full), Medicine check, Healing

### Exhausted
**Cause**: Lack of rest, overexertion, environmental
**Effect**: Disadvantage on all actions until rested
**Recovery**: Long rest required

### Stunned
**Cause**: Critical hit, special abilities, overwhelming force
**Effect**: Lose next action
**Recovery**: Automatic after one turn

### Prone
**Cause**: Knocked down, tripped, fall
**Effect**:
- Disadvantage on attacks
- Enemies have advantage (melee) or disadvantage (ranged)
**Recovery**: Costs 1 action to stand

### Restrained/Grappled
**Cause**: Grabbed by enemy, trapped, environmental
**Effect**:
- Cannot move
- Disadvantage on attacks
- Attackers have advantage
**Recovery**: Athletics or Acrobatics check (DC 10) to escape

### Blinded
**Cause**: Darkness, blinding effects, covering eyes
**Effect**:
- Disadvantage on attacks
- Enemies have advantage against you
- Cannot perceive visual information
**Recovery**: Remove source, healing, time

### Deafened
**Cause**: Loud noise, covering ears, damage
**Effect**:
- Cannot hear warnings or sounds
- -2 to Perception checks
**Recovery**: Remove source, healing, time

---

## Mental/Social Conditions

### Shaken
**Cause**: Fear, intimidation, psychological trauma
**Effect**: Disadvantage on mental and social actions
**Recovery**: Reassurance, safe environment, time, Composure check

### Frightened
**Cause**: Terror, horror, overwhelming threat
**Effect**:
- Disadvantage on all actions while source visible
- Cannot willingly move closer to source
**Recovery**: Remove source, overcome fear (check), time

### Confused
**Cause**: Mind-affecting abilities, illusions, chaos
**Effect**: GM determines your actions (or roll randomly)
**Recovery**: Clear thinking, time, allies help

### Charmed
**Cause**: Social manipulation, magic, supernatural
**Effect**:
- View charmer as friendly
- Cannot attack charmer
- Charmer has advantage on social checks
**Recovery**: Charmer attacks you, time expires, broken by ally

---

## Status Conditions

### Hindered
**Cause**: Difficult terrain, obstacles, impediments
**Effect**: Movement or specific actions restricted
**Recovery**: Overcome obstacle, change environment

### Exposed
**Cause**: No cover, tactical disadvantage, vulnerability
**Effect**: Enemies have advantage to attack you
**Recovery**: Move to cover, change position

### Slowed
**Cause**: Environmental effects, abilities, conditions
**Effect**: Movement costs double actions or is halved
**Recovery**: Remove source, overcome effect

---

## Beneficial Conditions

### Empowered
**Cause**: Magic, gear, circumstance, ally assistance
**Effect**: Advantage on relevant actions
**Duration**: Varies (GM determines)

### Inspired
**Cause**: Ally assistance, stirring speech, perfect moment
**Effect**: Advantage on next check or +2 bonus
**Duration**: One action or scene

### Hidden
**Cause**: Stealth, concealment, invisibility
**Effect**:
- Enemies cannot target you directly
- Advantage on attacks from hiding
**Lost when**: You attack, make noise, are discovered

### Defended
**Cause**: Cover, defensive stance, ally protection
**Effect**: +2 to defense or impose disadvantage on attacks
**Duration**: Until you move or stance ends

## Damage Types & Resistance

### Wounded (damage-based)
**Minor** (1-3 HP): Scratches, bruises
**Moderate** (4-6 HP): Cuts, impacts
**Serious** (7-9 HP): Deep wounds, breaks
**Critical** (10+ HP): Life-threatening

### Resistance
**Effect**: Take half damage from source
**Sources**: Armor, abilities, environmental

### Vulnerability
**Effect**: Take double damage from source
**Sources**: Weaknesses, specific damage types

---

## Condition Stacking

**Same Condition**: Usually doesn't stack (use worst)
**Different Conditions**: Stack normally
**Multiple Disadvantages**: Still just disadvantage (doesn't stack)
**Advantage + Disadvantage**: Cancel out (straight roll)

---

## Removing Conditions

### Immediate
- **Stunned**: Ends after one turn
- **Prone**: Stand as action
- **Hidden**: Ends when discovered/attacked

### Short Rest (Scene)
- **Wounded** (partial recovery)
- **Exhausted** (reduce severity)
- **Minor conditions** (GM discretion)

### Long Rest (Session End)
- **Wounded** (significant recovery)
- **Exhausted** (full recovery)
- **Most conditions** clear

### Specific Actions
- **Medicine check**: Remove Wounded
- **Reassurance**: Remove Shaken
- **Escape check**: Remove Restrained
- **Overcome fear**: Remove Frightened

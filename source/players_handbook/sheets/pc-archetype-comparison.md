### Cozy Archetypes (Chapter 20)

**Combat**: Guard Captain - Protection, law enforcement

**Technical**: Master Artisan - Crafting, building, repairing
**Stealth**: Village Scout - Tracking, gathering intelligence
**Support**: Healer/Herbalist - Medicine, brewing, care

### Fantasy Archetypes (Chapter 20)

**Combat**: Knight/Warrior - Honorable combat, protection
**Social**: Bard/Diplomat - Performance, influence, information
**Technical**: Artificer - Magical crafting, invention
**Stealth**: Rogue/Thief - Infiltration, acquisition, deception
**Support**: Cleric/Druid - Divine/nature magic, healing
**Magic**: Wizard/Sorcerer - Arcane power, knowledge

### Horror/Mystery Archetypes (Chapter 20)

**Combat**: Hunter - Monster slaying, occult combat

## Quick Selection Guide

### Choose Combat-Focused If You Like:
- Direct confrontation and tactical challenges
- Protecting others and fighting threats
- **Skills**: Combat, Athletics, Tactics
- **Proficiencies**: Weapons, Armor, Combat Techniques

### Choose Social/Diplomatic If You Like:
- Negotiation and influence
- Building relationships and networks
- **Skills**: Persuasion, Deception, Insight
- **Proficiencies**: Etiquette, Languages, Social Customs

### Choose Technical/Crafting If You Like:
- Problem-solving through invention
- Creating useful items and solutions
- **Skills**: Crafting, Mechanics, Lore
- **Proficiencies**: Tools, Technical Knowledge

### Choose Stealth/Subterfuge If You Like:
- Infiltration and information gathering
- Avoiding direct confrontation
- **Skills**: Stealth, Sleight of Hand, Deception
- **Proficiencies**: Lockpicking, Disguise, Infiltration

### Choose Support/Healing If You Like:
- Helping teammates succeed
- Keeping party healthy and functional
- **Skills**: Medicine, Empathy, Healing Arts
- **Proficiencies**: Medical Tools, Herbalism, First Aid

### Choose Exploration/Survival If You Like:
- Discovering new places
- Overcoming environmental challenges
- **Skills**: Survival, Navigation, Tracking
- **Proficiencies**: Wilderness Lore, Orienteering

### Choose Magic/Supernatural If You Like:
- Bending reality through power
- Solving problems with supernatural abilities
- **Skills**: Spellcraft, Ritual, Occult Knowledge
- **Proficiencies**: Magical Traditions, Rituals

### Choose Investigation If You Like:
- Solving mysteries and puzzles
- Piecing together clues
- **Skills**: Investigation, Perception, Deduction
- **Proficiencies**: Forensics, Research, Analysis

## Mixing Archetypes

**Not limited to one archetype!** Your character can:
- Start with one archetype's suggested skills
- Add skills from another archetype as you progress
- Combine elements to create hybrid concepts

**Examples**:
- Combat + Social = Charismatic Warrior
- Technical + Stealth = Hacker/Infiltrator
- Support + Investigation = Forensic Medic
- Magic + Combat = Battle Mage

# Backstory Integration Matrix

Connect your backstory to mechanical benefits and story hooks.

---

## Backstory Templates (Chapter 22)

### 1. The Seeker
**Concept**: Searching for lost person/thing/truth  
**Hook**: Clues appear, rival seekers, false leads  
**Skill Bonus**: Investigation or relevant knowledge  
**Connection**: To NPCs who might know something

### 2. The Fugitive
**Concept**: Running from past/organization/fate  
**Hook**: Pursuers arrive, past catches up  
**Skill Bonus**: Stealth or Deception  
**Connection**: To those who hide or hunt fugitives

### 3. The Avenger
**Concept**: Seeking justice/revenge for wrong  
**Hook**: Target appears, moral complications  
**Skill Bonus**: Combat or Investigation  
**Connection**: To enemy faction or victims

### 4. The Outcast
**Concept**: Rejected by home/group, seeking belonging  
**Hook**: Chance for redemption or acceptance  
**Skill Bonus**: Survival or relevant social  
**Connection**: To other outcasts or home community

### 5. The Chosen
**Concept**: Destiny/prophecy/special purpose  
**Hook**: Prophecy unfolds, doubts arise  
**Skill Bonus**: Relevant to destiny  
**Connection**: To those who believe/oppose destiny

### 6. The Protector
**Concept**: Sworn to defend person/place/ideal  
**Hook**: Ward endangered, loyalty tested  
**Skill Bonus**: Combat or relevant protective  
**Connection**: To those you protect

### 7. The Reformer
**Concept**: Trying to change self/system/world  
**Hook**: Opportunity or setback for change  
**Skill Bonus**: Persuasion or relevant skill  
**Connection**: To allies and opposition

### 8. The Survivor
**Concept**: Endured trauma, carries scars  
**Hook**: Trauma resurfaces, chance to heal  
**Skill Bonus**: Relevant to survival  
**Connection**: To others affected by same event

## Integration Checklist

**For each backstory element, ask**:

☐ **How does this create story hooks?**  
   What can GM use to create scenes?

☐ **Who does this connect me to?**  
   NPCs, factions, places?

☐ **What mechanical benefit?**  
   Skill, proficiency, item, contact?

☐ **When does this matter?**  
   What situations trigger this?

## Backstory to Mechanics

### Skills from Background
**Examples**:
- Soldier → Combat, Tactics
- Scholar → Lore, Investigation
- Criminal → Stealth, Deception
- Healer → Medicine, Empathy
- Merchant → Persuasion, Appraisal

### Proficiencies from Background
**Examples**:
- Guild member → Trade tools
- Noble → Etiquette, Languages
- Wilderness survivor → Survival gear
- Arcane student → Magical traditions

### Contacts from Background
**Examples**:
- Old mentor who teaches
- Rival who competes
- Family member who needs help
- Former ally now enemy
- Organization with resources

### Items from Background
**Examples**:
- Family heirloom (signature item)
- Tool of former trade
- Token from important event
- Map/clue to goal
- Gift from mentor

## Story Hook Integration

### Personal Hooks (Your Story)
**What you want**: Goal drives your actions  
**What you hide**: Secret creates complications  
**Who matters**: Relationships create drama

### Campaign Hooks (Group Story)
**Shared enemy**: Common threat  
**Shared goal**: Mutual objective  
**Shared past**: Connected backstories

### Session Hooks (Immediate)
**Clue appears**: Lead to personal goal  
**Contact arrives**: NPC from past  
**Complication**: Secret threatens exposure

## Integrating With Party

**Connect backstories through**:

**Shared Location**: From same place/event  
**Shared Person**: Know same NPC  
**Shared Goal**: Want similar things  
**Shared Enemy**: Opposed by same threat  
**Shared Experience**: Survived same trauma

**Example Connections**:
- We both trained under same mentor
- We're both hunting same enemy
- We both survived same disaster
- We both seek same artifact
- We're both fugitives from same organization

## GM Collaboration

**Share with GM**:
1. Your backstory template and hooks
2. NPCs you want to exist
3. Locations important to you
4. Factions you're connected to
5. Secrets you're keeping

**Ask GM**:
- How does my backstory fit the campaign?
- Which NPCs exist already?
- When might my hooks appear?
- What secrets should I keep/reveal?

# PC Template Selection Guide

Quick guide for selecting PC templates and kits (Chapter 21).

---

## What Are Templates?

**Templates** are pre-configured character starting points focused on specific roles or playstyles. They provide:
- Suggested skill combinations
- Recommended proficiencies
- Starting gear packages
- Character concept inspiration

**Use templates when**: You want a quick start or need inspiration
**Skip templates when**: You prefer building from scratch

---

## Template Categories

### Combat Templates
**Frontline Fighter**: High HP, melee combat, protection
**Ranged Striker**: Distance combat, positioning, precision
**Tactical Commander**: Battlefield control, team support
**Skirmisher**: Mobile combat, hit-and-run

### Social Templates
**Face/Diplomat**: Negotiation, leadership, persuasion
**Investigator**: Information gathering, deduction
**Infiltrator**: Stealth, disguise, social manipulation
**Performer**: Entertainment, distraction, inspiration

### Technical Templates
**Crafter/Builder**: Creation, repair, invention
**Scholar/Researcher**: Knowledge, analysis, learning
**Healer/Medic**: Support, recovery, care
**Tech Specialist**: Systems, hacking, engineering

### Exploration Templates
**Scout/Ranger**: Wilderness, tracking, survival
**Urban Explorer**: City navigation, contacts
**Treasure Hunter**: Finding valuables, ancient sites
**Guide**: Navigation, environmental knowledge

## Template Modification

**Templates are starting points - customize freely!**

**Easy modifications**:
- Swap one skill for another
- Change proficiency to fit concept
- Adjust gear to match backstory
- Rename to fit your vision

**Examples**:
- "Frontline Fighter" → "Gentle Giant Protector"
- "Investigator" → "Conspiracy Theorist"
- "Scout" → "Urban Parkour Artist"

---

## Mixing Templates

**Combine elements from 2 templates**:

**Combat + Social**: Charismatic Warrior
- 2 combat skills + 2 social skills
- Mix combat and social proficiencies

**Technical + Exploration**: Engineer Scout
- 2 technical skills + 2 exploration skills
- Field repair + navigation proficiencies

**Social + Technical**: Con Artist Hacker
- 2 social skills + 2 technical skills
- Deception + hacking proficiencies

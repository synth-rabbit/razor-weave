## Quick Checklist

Before starting play, confirm you have:

- ☐ Name and basic identity
- ☐ One-sentence concept
- ☐ Three personality traits
- ☐ Clear goal (what you want)
- ☐ Compelling secret (what you hide)
- ☐ 3-5 skills chosen
- ☐ 2-4 proficiencies chosen
- ☐ Signature item defined
- ☐ 5-6 pieces of starting gear
- ☐ Brief backstory written
- ☐ 2-3 story hooks for GM
- ☐ 2-3 relationships established
- ☐ Character sheet filled out

---

**Now you're ready to play!**

Transfer all information to your **Blank Character Sheet** and bring your character to life!

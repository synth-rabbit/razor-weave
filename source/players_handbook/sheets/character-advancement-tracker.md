## Tier Progression

### Tier 1: Novice (Starting)
**Health**: 10 HP
**Skills**: 3-5 skills at +0 to +2
**Proficiencies**: 2-3 proficiencies
**Focus**: Learning basics, establishing identity

**Milestones to Tier 2**:
- ☐ Complete 3-5 significant sessions
- ☐ Achieve major story goal
- ☐ Demonstrate mastery of core abilities
- ☐ **OR** Earn 10-15 XP

### Tier 2: Competent
**Health**: 20 HP (+10)
**Skills**: Can advance to +3, add 1-2 new skills
**Proficiencies**: Add 1-2 new proficiencies
**Focus**: Specialization, building reputation

**Milestones to Tier 3**:
- ☐ Complete 5-7 more sessions
- ☐ Resolve major campaign arc
- ☐ Gain regional recognition
- ☐ **OR** Earn 15-20 additional XP

### Tier 3: Expert
**Health**: 30 HP (+10)
**Skills**: Can advance to +4, add 1-2 new skills
**Proficiencies**: Add 1-2 advanced proficiencies
**Focus**: Mastery, leadership, influence

**Milestones to Tier 4**:
- ☐ Complete 7-10 more sessions
- ☐ Overcome legendary challenge
- ☐ Gain national/world recognition
- ☐ **OR** Earn 20-25 additional XP

### Tier 4: Master
**Health**: 40 HP (+10)
**Skills**: Can advance to +5
**Proficiencies**: Add 1-2 legendary proficiencies
**Focus**: World-changing, mentoring others

### Tier 5: Legendary
**Health**: 50 HP (+10)
**Skills**: Peak mastery, potentially beyond +5
**Proficiencies**: Unique legendary abilities
**Focus**: Myth-making, legacy

## Quick Reference Checklist

**Before Every Session**:
- ☐ Review character sheet
- ☐ Check last session notes
- ☐ Know your current goal
- ☐ Prepare questions for GM
- ☐ Bring dice, pencil, character sheet

**During Every Session**:
- ☐ Take notes on important info
- ☐ Track resources/HP changes
- ☐ Note new NPCs and locations
- ☐ Ask questions when confused
- ☐ Support other players' moments

**After Every Session**:
- ☐ Update character sheet
- ☐ Note unresolved threads
- ☐ Set goal for next session
- ☐ Thank GM and other players

---

## Tips for Better Sessions

**Engagement**:
- Come prepared with ideas
- Build on others' actions
- Ask questions in character
- Share spotlight with others

**Note-Taking**:
- Focus on names and key facts
- Use shorthand for quick notes
- Review notes between sessions
- Share information with party

**Character Development**:
- Look for growth opportunities
- Connect to other characters
- Follow your goals consistently
- Let character change over time

**Table Etiquette**:
- Stay focused during others' turns
- Avoid side conversations
- Support GM's rulings
- Contribute to collaborative story

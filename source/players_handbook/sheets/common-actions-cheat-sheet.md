# Common Actions Cheat Sheet

Quick reference for the most common actions players take during play.

---

## Making a Check

**When to roll**: The GM calls for a check when the outcome is uncertain and failure is interesting.

**How to roll**:
1. **Roll 2d6** + relevant skill bonus
2. **Compare to DC** (Difficulty Class set by GM)
3. **Interpret result**:
   - **10+**: Full success
   - **7-9**: Partial success (success with cost/complication)
   - **6 or less**: Failure (but story moves forward)

**Advantage/Disadvantage**:
- **Advantage**: Roll **3d6**, keep highest 2
- **Disadvantage**: Roll **3d6**, keep lowest 2

## Combat Actions

### On Your Turn (choose 2 actions)

**Move**: Change position, range, or tactical situation

**Attack**: Deal damage or create threat
- Roll relevant combat skill vs. DC
- Success: Deal damage or apply condition
- Partial: Deal damage but expose yourself

**Defend**: Protect yourself or an ally
- Gain defensive position or cover
- Impose disadvantage on enemy attacks

**Use Item**: Activate gear, consume resource, interact with environment

**Help**: Grant advantage to an ally's next action

**Special Action**: Use proficiency, environmental feature, or creative tactic

### Reactions (interrupt enemy actions)

**Dodge**: When targeted, impose disadvantage on attack

**Counter**: When hit, attempt to counter-attack (costs your next action)

**Protect**: Interpose yourself to shield an ally

## Exploration Actions

**Investigate**: Examine clues, search area, gather information
- **DC 8**: Obvious details
- **DC 10**: Hidden but findable
- **DC 12+**: Obscure or well-hidden

**Navigate**: Chart course, track quarry, find path
- **DC 8**: Clear terrain, obvious route
- **DC 10**: Moderate challenge
- **DC 12+**: Difficult terrain, hidden paths

**Interact**: Manipulate environment, use tools, activate mechanisms
- **DC 8**: Simple, designed for use
- **DC 10**: Moderate complexity
- **DC 12+**: Complex, unusual, or damaged

---

## Social Actions

**Persuade**: Convince, negotiate, inspire
- **DC 8**: Already inclined to agree
- **DC 10**: Neutral, needs convincing
- **DC 12+**: Hostile or opposed

**Deceive**: Lie, bluff, misdirect
- **DC 8**: Target has no reason to doubt
- **DC 10**: Target is cautious
- **DC 12+**: Target is suspicious

**Intimidate**: Threaten, coerce, display dominance
- **DC 8**: Already frightened or vulnerable
- **DC 10**: Neutral, can be swayed by force
- **DC 12+**: Confident or defiant

**Read**: Assess motives, detect lies, gauge intentions
- **DC 8**: Obvious tells, strong emotion
- **DC 10**: Subtle but present
- **DC 12+**: Practiced liar, hidden motives

---

## Downtime Actions

**Recover**: Rest to heal health and reduce stress
- Short rest (scene): Regain small amount
- Long rest (session): Regain significant amount

**Train**: Practice skills, learn proficiencies
- Requires time investment and opportunity

**Pursue Goal**: Work toward personal objective
- Advance backstory hook or character arc

**Build Relationship**: Deepen bonds with NPCs or PCs
- Create allies, gain favors, unlock opportunities

## Quick DC Reference

| Difficulty | DC | Examples |
|------------|----|----|
| **Easy** | 6 | Routine task under ideal conditions |
| **Moderate** | 8 | Standard challenge, some risk |
| **Challenging** | 10 | Difficult but achievable with skill |
| **Hard** | 12 | Requires expertise or luck |
| **Very Hard** | 14 | Near-impossible without mastery |
| **Heroic** | 16+ | Legendary feat, extraordinary circumstances |

**Modifiers**:
- **+2**: Favorable circumstances, clever approach
- **-2**: Unfavorable circumstances, poor position
- **Advantage**: Preparation, help from ally, ideal conditions
- **Disadvantage**: Rushed, injured, opposed by enemy

---

## Common Conditions (Effects)

**Wounded**: Reduced health, disadvantage on physical actions until healed

**Exhausted**: Reduced stamina, disadvantage on all actions until rested

**Shaken**: Reduced composure, disadvantage on mental/social actions until calmed

**Hindered**: Movement or actions restricted by environment or effect

**Exposed**: Vulnerable to attacks, enemies gain advantage against you

**Empowered**: Enhanced by magic, gear, or circumstance - gain advantage

---

## Help Your Fellow Players

**Grant Advantage**: Describe how you assist before they roll

**Create Opportunity**: Use your action to set up ally's success

**Share Resources**: Lend gear, provide healing, offer information

**Play Into Backstory**: Engage with their goals, secrets, and relationships

**Celebrate Success**: Highlight cool moments and clever solutions

---

## When in Doubt

**Ask Questions**: "What do I see?", "What's my character feeling?", "What are my options?"

**Declare Intent**: Describe what you want to accomplish, GM determines the mechanic

**Fiction First**: Describe your action in the world, then roll dice

**Embrace Failure**: Failure creates drama and opportunities - lean into it

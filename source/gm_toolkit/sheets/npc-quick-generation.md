# NPC Quick Generation Table

Roll or choose to create NPCs on the fly during play.

---

## 5-Minute NPC Generation

**Step 1**: Roll 2d6 or choose from each section
**Step 2**: Combine results into coherent NPC
**Step 3**: Add name and you're done!

---

## NPC Role (2d6)

| Roll | Role | Function |
|------|------|----------|
| 2-3 | **Ally** | Supports PCs, provides resources/information |
| 4-5 | **Informant** | Has valuable information, wants something in return |
| 6-7 | **Neutral/Bystander** | Caught in situation, reacts to PCs |
| 8-9 | **Rival** | Competes with PCs for same goals |
| 10-11 | **Antagonist** | Opposes PCs with conflicting agenda |
| 12 | **Mentor** | Guides PCs, has limitations preventing direct action |

---

## Mannerism (2d6)

| Roll | Mannerism | Physical Tell |
|------|-----------|---------------|
| 2 | **Fidgets constantly** | Taps fingers, adjusts clothing, paces |
| 3 | **Speaks in whispers** | Leans close, secretive tone |
| 4 | **Avoids eye contact** | Looks away, studies ground, distracted |
| 5 | **Laughs nervously** | Inappropriate timing, awkward giggles |
| 6 | **Very formal** | Proper speech, rigid posture, ceremonial |
| 7 | **Casual/relaxed** | Slouches, informal language, comfortable |
| 8 | **Uses gestures** | Animated hands, expressive body language |
| 9 | **Touches face/hair** | Adjusts glasses, tucks hair, strokes beard |
| 10 | **Direct stare** | Intense eye contact, unblinking, confrontational |
| 11 | **Smells things** | Sniffs air, inspects food, sensory-focused |
| 12 | **Hums/whistles** | Musical, rhythmic, often distracted |

## Goal (What They Want Right Now)

| Roll | Goal | Motivation |
|------|------|------------|
| 2 | **Survive** | Immediate danger, protect self/loved ones |
| 3 | **Get rich** | Money, treasure, valuable item or information |
| 4 | **Gain power** | Authority, influence, control over situation |
| 5 | **Find truth** | Solve mystery, uncover secret, learn answers |
| 6 | **Seek revenge** | Punish wrongdoer, settle old score |
| 7 | **Protect someone** | Keep person/place safe from threat |
| 8 | **Escape situation** | Leave town, avoid consequences, flee danger |
| 9 | **Prove themselves** | Gain respect, demonstrate worth, achieve recognition |
| 10 | **Fix problem** | Solve practical issue, repair damage, restore order |
| 11 | **Gain approval** | Please authority figure, earn trust, fit in |
| 12 | **Hide secret** | Conceal truth, avoid exposure, maintain deception |

## Secret (What They're Hiding)

| Roll | Secret | Complication |
|------|--------|--------------|
| 2 | **Working for enemy** | Double agent, coerced, or true believer |

| 6 | **Different identity** | Disguised, assumed name, hiding past |
| 7 | **Romantic entanglement** | Secret relationship complicates loyalties |
| 8 | **Made terrible mistake** | Past error haunts them, fear of exposure |
| 9 | **Supernatural connection** | Cursed, blessed, prophesied, haunted |
| 10 | **Terminal condition** | Dying, cursed, limited time |
| 11 | **Protecting someone** | Hiding fugitive, shielding loved one |
| 12 | **Knows location** | Treasure, hideout, secret passage |

## Quick Skills (If Combat Likely)

**Tier 1 NPCs**: 1-2 skills at +1
**Tier 2 NPCs**: 2-3 skills at +1 or +2
**Tier 3+ NPCs**: 3-4 skills at +2

**Common NPC Skills**:
- **Combat**: Melee, Ranged, Tactics
- **Social**: Persuasion, Deception, Intimidation
- **Knowledge**: Lore, Investigation, Perception
- **Practical**: Crafting, Medicine, Survival

## Example Generated NPCs

### Example 1: Quick Ally
**Roll**: Role=3 (Ally), Trait=7 (Friendly), Mannerism=8 (Gestures), Goal=7 (Protect someone), Secret=11 (Protecting someone)
**Result**: **Hazel** - Friendly shopkeeper who uses animated gestures when speaking. Wants to protect her nephew from local gang. Secret: She's hiding him in her basement from debt collectors.
**Skills**: Persuasion +1, Local Knowledge +1

### Example 2: Mysterious Informant
**Roll**: Role=4 (Informant), Trait=8 (Suspicious), Mannerism=3 (Whispers), Goal=3 (Get rich), Secret=3 (Knows important fact)
**Result**: **Marcus** - Suspicious informant who speaks in whispers. Wants to sell valuable information about murder he witnessed. Keeps glancing over shoulder.
**Skills**: Stealth +1, Perception +2, Streetwise +1

### Example 3: Dangerous Rival
**Roll**: Role=9 (Rival), Trait=11 (Arrogant), Mannerism=10 (Direct stare), Goal=9 (Prove themselves), Secret=2 (Working for enemy)
**Result**: **Captain Valen** - Arrogant rival hunter with intimidating direct stare. Wants to prove superior by beating PCs to bounty. Secretly reports their movements to crime lord.
**Skills**: Tracking +2, Combat +2, Intimidation +2

## Promoting NPCs

**If NPC becomes important**, spend 10 minutes expanding:
1. Add 2-3 more personality traits
2. Define relationships to other NPCs/factions
3. Add 1-2 more skills
4. Expand secret into mini-backstory
5. Give them a character arc potential

# Enemy Difficulty Calculator

Quick tool for creating enemies at appropriate difficulty levels.

---

## Enemy Power Levels

### Minion (Weak)
**Purpose**: Easily defeated, create numbers

**Stats**:
- **HP**: 1-3
- **Skills**: +0 to +1
- **Attack**: Weak (DC 8)
- **Defense**: Easy to hit (DC 8)

**Tactics**: Swarm, distract, die easily

---

### Standard (Equal)
**Purpose**: Match PC capabilities, core encounters

**By Tier**:

**Tier 1**:
- **HP**: 8-12
- **Skills**: +1 to +2
- **Attack**: Standard (DC 10)
- **Defense**: Standard (DC 10)

**Tier 2**:
- **HP**: 12-16
- **Skills**: +2 to +3
- **Attack**: Challenging (DC 10-11)
- **Defense**: Challenging (DC 10-11)

**Tier 3**:
- **HP**: 16-20
- **Skills**: +3 to +4
- **Attack**: Hard (DC 11-12)
- **Defense**: Hard (DC 11-12)

**Tier 4**:
- **HP**: 20-25
- **Skills**: +4 to +5
- **Attack**: Very Hard (DC 12-13)
- **Defense**: Very Hard (DC 12-13)

---

### Elite (Strong)
**Purpose**: Significant threat, mini-bosses

**By Tier**:

**Tier 1**:
- **HP**: 15-20
- **Skills**: +2 to +3
- **Attack**: Challenging (DC 11)
- **Defense**: Challenging (DC 11)
- **Special**: 1 special ability

**Tier 2**:
- **HP**: 20-28
- **Skills**: +3 to +4
- **Attack**: Hard (DC 12)
- **Defense**: Hard (DC 12)
- **Special**: 2 special abilities

**Tier 3**:
- **HP**: 28-36
- **Skills**: +4 to +5
- **Attack**: Very Hard (DC 13)
- **Defense**: Very Hard (DC 13)
- **Special**: 2-3 special abilities

**Tier 4**:
- **HP**: 35-45
- **Skills**: +5 to +6
- **Attack**: Extreme (DC 14)
- **Defense**: Extreme (DC 14)
- **Special**: 3-4 special abilities

---

### Boss (Legendary)
**Purpose**: Major encounters, campaign climaxes

**By Tier**:

**Tier 1**:
- **HP**: 25-35
- **Skills**: +3 to +4
- **Attack**: Hard (DC 12)
- **Defense**: Hard (DC 12)
- **Actions**: 2 per turn OR reactions
- **Special**: 2-3 special abilities
- **Phases**: Optional 2 phases

**Tier 2**:
- **HP**: 35-50
- **Skills**: +4 to +5
- **Attack**: Very Hard (DC 13)
- **Defense**: Very Hard (DC 13)
- **Actions**: 2 per turn + reactions
- **Special**: 3-4 special abilities
- **Phases**: 2-3 phases recommended

**Tier 3**:
- **HP**: 50-70
- **Skills**: +5 to +6
- **Attack**: Extreme (DC 14)
- **Defense**: Extreme (DC 14)
- **Actions**: 2-3 per turn + reactions
- **Special**: 4-5 special abilities
- **Phases**: 3 phases recommended

**Tier 4**:
- **HP**: 70-100
- **Skills**: +6 to +7
- **Attack**: Nearly Impossible (DC 15)
- **Defense**: Nearly Impossible (DC 15)
- **Actions**: 3 per turn + reactions
- **Special**: 5+ special abilities
- **Phases**: 3-4 phases

## Special Ability Templates

### Offensive Abilities

**Area Attack**:
- Target multiple PCs in zone
- Each makes check or takes damage/condition
- Cooldown: 2-3 rounds

**Devastating Strike**:
- One target, massive damage
- Requires setup or telegraph
- Once per combat or limited uses

**Status Effect**:
- Impose condition on hit (Wounded, Stunned, etc.)
- Save to negate or short duration
- Varies by enemy type

**Summon Minions**:
- Call 2-4 weak allies
- Once per combat or on threshold
- Minions act on enemy turn

---

### Defensive Abilities

**Armor/Shield**:
- Reduce incoming damage
- OR advantage on defense
- Always active or limited

**Regeneration**:
- Recover HP each round
- Stops if certain condition met
- 2-5 HP per round based on tier

**Resistance**:
- Half damage from type
- OR immunity to condition
- Always active

**Evasion**:
- Reaction to dodge attack
- Limited uses per combat
- Move or negate hit

---

### Control Abilities

**Grapple/Restrain**:
- Prevents target movement
- Check to escape
- Can attack while maintained

**Fear/Intimidation**:
- Target disadvantaged or flees
- Save to resist
- Radius effect or single target

**Terrain Manipulation**:
- Create hazards or obstacles
- Lasts rounds or until destroyed
- Affects positioning

**Mind Control**:
- Force actions or suggestions
- Very limited duration
- Boss-level threat only

---

### Support Abilities

**Buff Allies**:
- Grant advantage or bonus
- Radius or specific targets
- Lasts rounds or until broken

**Heal Allies**:
- Restore HP
- Limited uses
- Usually Elite+ only

**Coordinate**:
- Allies act more effectively
- Tactical bonuses
- Leadership role

## Enemy Roles

### Striker
**Purpose**: Deal damage
**High**: Attack skill, damage output
**Low**: HP, defense
**Abilities**: Devastating attacks, mobility
**Tactics**: Hit hard, avoid hits

---

### Tank
**Purpose**: Absorb damage, protect allies
**High**: HP, defense
**Low**: Attack power, speed
**Abilities**: Armor, regeneration, taunts
**Tactics**: Front line, draw fire

---

### Support
**Purpose**: Aid allies
**High**: Utility, positioning
**Low**: Personal combat power
**Abilities**: Buffs, healing, summons
**Tactics**: Back line, survive to help

---

### Controller
**Purpose**: Limit PC options
**High**: Area effects, conditions
**Low**: Single-target power
**Abilities**: Restraints, terrain, debuffs
**Tactics**: Disrupt, control space

## Quick Enemy Scaling

### Make Easier
- ☐ Reduce HP by 25%
- ☐ Lower skill bonuses by 1
- ☐ Remove one special ability
- ☐ Reduce numbers

### Make Harder
- ☐ Increase HP by 25%
- ☐ Raise skill bonuses by 1
- ☐ Add special ability
- ☐ Add minions
- ☐ Better tactics

## Enemy by Genre

### Cozy
- Low HP, low threat
- Conflicts about resolve, not killing
- "Enemies" often misunderstood
- Focus on defense/escape

### Fantasy
- Traditional scaling
- Magic adds complexity
- Legendary creatures as bosses
- Monster variety

### Horror
- Lower HP, higher damage
- Dangerous even alone
- Emphasis on fear/escape
- Overwhelming when many

### Modern
- Realistic HP (guns hurt)
- Cover crucial
- Tactics over brute force
- Technology as edge

### Sci-Fi
- Variable scale (personal to ship)
- Tech provides special abilities
- Environmental hazards
- Alien logic

## Testing and Adjustment

**After combat, evaluate**:

**Too Easy**:
- Combat over in 2-3 rounds
- PCs barely scratched
- No tension

**Just Right**:
- 3-5 rounds
- Some resource expenditure
- Tension and tactics mattered

**Too Hard**:
- 6+ rounds slog
- Multiple PCs down
- Felt unfair

**Adjust future encounters based on results**

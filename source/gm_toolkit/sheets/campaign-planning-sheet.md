## Session Zero Prep Checklist

**Before Session Zero**:
- ☐ Complete campaign concept section
- ☐ Prepare campaign pitch (2-3 min)
- ☐ Decide on character creation guidelines
- ☐ Prepare safety tools discussion
- ☐ Draft schedule and logistics

**During Session Zero**:
- ☐ Present campaign pitch
- ☐ Discuss themes and tone
- ☐ Establish safety tools
- ☐ Create characters together
- ☐ Establish party connections
- ☐ Set expectations
- ☐ Schedule Session 1

**After Session Zero**:
- ☐ Integrate PC backstories into campaign
- ☐ Adjust plans based on character choices
- ☐ Prepare Session 1 with PC hooks
- ☐ Send recap and reminders

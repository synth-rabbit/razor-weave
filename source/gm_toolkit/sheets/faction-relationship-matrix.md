## Reputation System

### Reputation Levels

**0-2: Hostile**
- Actively oppose PCs
- Attack on sight or sabotage
- Refuse all interaction

**3-4: Wary**
- Suspicious and unhelpful
- Require proof of good intent
- Minimum cooperation

**5-6: Neutral**
- Standard interactions
- Transactional relationships
- No special treatment

**7-8: Friendly**
- Trust PCs
- Offer assistance
- Share some information

**9-10: Allied**
- Strong mutual support
- Share resources freely
- Trust with sensitive information

### Gaining/Losing Reputation

**+1 Reputation**:
- Complete faction mission
- Help faction member
- Advance faction goals
- Oppose faction enemy

**-1 Reputation**:
- Fail faction mission
- Harm faction member
- Oppose faction goals
- Aid faction enemy

**Instant Hostile**:
- Betray faction
- Kill faction leader
- Destroy faction asset

**Instant Allied**:
- Save faction from destruction
- Complete major campaign goal for faction
- Legendary heroic act

## Quick Reference: Faction Actions

### What Factions Do Each Session

**High reputation factions**:
- Offer missions
- Provide resources
- Share intelligence
- Request PC help

**Neutral factions**:
- Transactional interactions
- React to PC actions
- Pursue own goals

**Low reputation factions**:
- Create obstacles
- Oppose PC goals
- Spread misinformation
- Attack when opportune

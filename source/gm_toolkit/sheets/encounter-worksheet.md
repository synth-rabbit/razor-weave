## Quick Encounter Templates

### Simple Combat
- **Participants**: 1 group of enemies
- **Location**: Simple terrain
- **Goal**: Defeat enemies
- **Duration**: 15-20 minutes

### Complex Combat
- **Participants**: Mixed enemy types
- **Location**: Dynamic terrain
- **Goal**: Multiple objectives
- **Complications**: Environment, time, protection
- **Duration**: 30-45 minutes

### Social Negotiation
- **Participants**: 1-3 NPCs
- **Location**: Appropriate setting
- **Goal**: Reach agreement
- **Complications**: Competing interests, secrets
- **Duration**: 15-30 minutes

### Exploration/Investigation
- **Challenge**: Find/discover something
- **Clues**: Multiple paths to success
- **Hazards**: Optional complications
- **Duration**: 15-30 minutes

---

## Genre-Specific Notes

### Cozy
- Focus on resolve over violence
- Environmental challenges over combat
- Community impact emphasized
- Positive resolutions preferred

### Fantasy
- Magic creates complications
- Legendary creatures as set pieces
- Heroic moments emphasized
- Clear good vs. evil

### Horror/Mystery
- Tension over action
- Information as reward
- Escape often better than fight
- Consequences emphasized

### Modern
- Cover and tactics crucial
- Technology as tool
- Realistic consequences
- Gray morality

### Sci-Fi
- Scale varies wildly
- Technology complications
- Environmental hazards
- First contact protocols

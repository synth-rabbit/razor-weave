## DC Ladder & Outcome Tiers {#dc-ladder--outcome-tiers}

DC cues

- 12 Easy: safe, aided, trivial with time
- 14 Routine: standard challenge, normal tools
- 16 Tough: notable risk, bad angle, poor tools
- 18 Hard: steep odds, strong opposition, tight window
- 20 Heroic: exceptional, stacked against, slim margin
- 22 Legendary: near impossible without setup or luck

Outcome tiers

- Full: objective achieved cleanly; tick progress or gain position
- Partial: progress with cost; fill pre‑stated pressure or apply a tag
- Fail: no progress; escalate danger; reveal a new obstacle or cost
- Crits: amplify change; consider clearing a small tag on crit success

## Gameplay Loop & Scene Procedure {#gameplay-loop--scene-procedure}

Loop

- Ask → Declare → Judge → Resolve → Update → Ask
- Pre‑state stakes; tie fiction to numbers; keep state visible

Scene procedure

- Frame → Stakes → Check → Narrate → React
- Name 1–2 tags that matter; show levers to flip them
- On Partial, move the fiction (tick pressure, apply tag); avoid stalls

## Tags & Conditions {#tags--conditions}

Tags (space levers)

- Dim/Obscuring: favors stealth; hinders navigation or ranged checks
- Slick/Cramped: hinders forced movement; increases risk of falls
- Fragile/Solid Cover: degrades on Partial; protects on Full

Conditions (state changes)

- Exhausted: reduce effectiveness until rest/aid
- Restrained: limit movement; clear via help, time, or leverage
- Bleeding: time pressure; clear via first aid or rest

GM usage

- Add 1–2 meaningful tags per scene; make effects explicit
- Offer clear ways to clear/mitigate conditions

## Group Checks & Contests {#group-checks--contests}

Group checks

- Majority: succeed if most do; Partial if split
- All‑Must‑Succeed: use when a single failure breaks the attempt
- Leader‑with‑Help: leader rolls; up to +2 aid dice from helpers

Contests

- Both sides roll; higher wins; ties are Partial by default
- Pre‑state results for each side

## Combat Cheat Sheet {#combat-cheat-sheet}

Ordering

- Conversational or Popcorn; rotate spotlight; break ties by danger/sense

Core actions

- Set Up: create angle/space; reduce DCs; apply tags
- Strike: contest vs defender or passive DC
- Maneuver: shove, trip, disarm, reposition; trade space/position on Partial
- Defend/Withdraw: change position/effect; clear a minor tag; break contact

Terrain levers

- Elevation, Cover (Fragile/Solid), Dim/Obscuring, Slick/Cramped
- On Partial, degrade cover or tick pressure as telegraphed

## Rulings Checklist {#rulings-checklist}

- Say stakes: Full/Partial/Fail before dice
- Choose DC from fiction; note Advantage/Disadvantage from tags/position
- Map tier to fiction; move forward on Partial/Fail
- Update state: tick clocks, add/remove tags, impose/clear conditions
- Ask again and rotate spotlight

# Proficiency Catalog (All Genres)

Comprehensive list of proficiencies across all genres.

---

## How Proficiencies Work

**Proficiency** = Specialized training or expertise

**Mechanical Effect**:
- +1 or +2 bonus to relevant checks
- OR advantage on checks
- OR enables attempt at specialized task

**When to Apply**:
- Task specifically matches proficiency
- Player declares use
- Makes fictional sense

## Creating Custom Proficiencies

### Guidelines

**Good Proficiency**:
- Specific enough to be meaningful
- Broad enough to use regularly
- Enables cool character moments
- Fits character concept

**Too Broad**:
- "Combat" (use skills instead)
- "Magic" (too general)
- "Technology" (what aspect?)

**Too Narrow**:
- "Opening red doors"
- "Fighting only goblins"
- "Tuesday negotiations"

**Just Right**:
- "Lockpicking"
- "Dragon lore"
- "Starship piloting"
- "Forensic analysis"

## Proficiency Bonus Values

### +1 Bonus
**Standard proficiency**:
- Represents training
- Applies regularly
- Most common

### +2 Bonus
**Master proficiency**:
- Represents expertise
- Requires special investment
- Rare, requires GM approval

### Advantage
**Alternative to bonus**:
- When proficiency exceptionally relevant
- One-time boost
- At GM discretion

### Enables Attempt
**Some proficiencies required**:
- Can't pick locks without proficiency
- Can't pilot starship without training
- Can't perform surgery untrained

---

## Proficiency Selection

### At Character Creation
**Typical number**: 2-4 proficiencies
- Based on background
- Reflects training and life experience
- Should support character concept

### Gaining New Proficiencies
**During play**:
- Downtime training
- Milestone advancement
- Story-driven acquisition
- Finding teachers/mentors

## Using Proficiencies in Play

### When Player Declares
**PC**: "I want to pick the lock using my lockpicking proficiency"
**GM**: "Great, roll Thievery +2 for the proficiency"

### When GM Suggests
**GM**: "You need to navigate through the storm. Anyone have sailing proficiency?"
**PC**: "I do! Does that help?"
**GM**: "Yes, advantage on the check"

### When Enables Attempt
**PC**: "I want to hack the computer"
**GM**: "Do you have hacking proficiency?"
**PC**: "No"
**GM**: "Then you can try but it's disadvantage/higher DC"

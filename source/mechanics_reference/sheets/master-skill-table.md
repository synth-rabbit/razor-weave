## Quick Selection by Archetype

### Combat-Focused Characters
**Pick 2-3 from**: Relevant combat skills + Athletics + Perception
**Example** (Fantasy): Melee Combat +2, Ranged Combat +1, Athletics +1, Perception +1

### Social/Diplomatic Characters
**Pick 2-3 from**: Persuasion/Diplomacy + Insight/Reading People + Knowledge
**Example** (Modern): Negotiation +2, Psychology +1, Investigation +1

### Technical/Crafting Characters
**Pick 2-3 from**: Primary craft + Related knowledge + Perception
**Example** (Sci-Fi): Engineering +2, Robotics +1, Analysis +1

### Stealth/Infiltration Characters
**Pick 2-3 from**: Stealth + Social deception + Investigation
**Example** (Horror): Stealth +2, Deception +1, Investigation +1

### Support/Healing Characters
**Pick 2-3 from**: Medicine/Healing + Empathy + Knowledge
**Example** (Cozy): Medicine +2, Empathy +1, Herbalism +1

### Investigation Characters
**Pick 2-3 from**: Investigation/Perception + Knowledge + Social
**Example** (Modern): Investigation +2, Forensics +1, Interrogation +1

## Skill Ratings

**Starting Characters** (Tier 1):
- Distribute **5 points** among 3-5 skills
- No skill above +2 at start
- Common distribution: +2, +1, +1, +1

**Progression**:
- **Tier 1**: Skills at +0 to +2
- **Tier 2**: Skills at +1 to +3
- **Tier 3**: Skills at +2 to +4
- **Tier 4+**: Skills at +3 to +5

---

## Combining Skills

**When tasks require multiple areas of expertise**, combine skills:

**Example Combinations**:
- **Combat + Acrobatics**: Flashy, mobile fighting
- **Stealth + Perception**: Careful infiltration
- **Knowledge + Social**: Teaching or convincing with expertise
- **Technical + Investigation**: Analyzing complex systems
- **Survival + Medicine**: Field medical care

**Mechanic**: Use higher skill, gain advantage if both relevant

---

## Porting Skills Between Genres

**To convert character to different genre**:

1. **Identify skill function** (combat, social, knowledge, etc.)
2. **Find equivalent in new genre** (use table above)
3. **Keep same ratings**
4. **Adjust flavor** to fit new setting

**Example**: Fantasy Ranger → Modern Survival Expert
- Survival (Fantasy) → Survival (Modern)
- Tracking (Fantasy) → Tracking (Modern)
- Ranged Combat (Fantasy) → Firearms (Modern)
- Nature Lore (Fantasy) → Wilderness Knowledge (Modern)

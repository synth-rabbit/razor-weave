What is Razorweave?

Razorweave is a tabletop roleplaying game designed for collaborative storytelling. The rules are simple, the narrative is central, and the mechanics get out of your way.

Core Philosophy

Fiction first. Always. The narrative determines what happens, and the mechanics support the story.

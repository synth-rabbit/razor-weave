# Clock Templates

Use these templates to track progress and pressure. Copy or redraw them on paper or a digital whiteboard.

## 4‑Segment Clock

Name: \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

Segments: ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐

Use for short tasks, immediate threats, or simple fronts.

## 6‑Segment Clock

Name: \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

Segments: ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐

Use for standard progress and threats that should take several beats to resolve.

## 8‑Segment Clock

Name: \_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_\_

Segments: ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐ ☐

Use for major arcs, long investigations, or slow‑burn fronts.

---

## Tips

- Write Clocks where everyone can see them.
- Label what happens when the Clock fills.
- Decide which Checks tick progress and which tick pressure.

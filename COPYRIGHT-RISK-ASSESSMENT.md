## 7. Game Mechanics Protection

### What You CANNOT Copyright:
- Rules and game mechanics
- Dice rolling methods
- Mathematical formulas
- Functional game systems
- Ideas and concepts

### What You CAN Copyright:
- **Expression of rules** (your specific wording)
- **Flavor text** (descriptions, examples)
- **Artwork and graphics**
- **Character/world fiction**
- **Specific tables/charts** (if creative expression involved)

### What You CAN Trademark:
- **"Razorweave"** name (consider registration)
- Logo/brand identity
- Taglines
- Product names

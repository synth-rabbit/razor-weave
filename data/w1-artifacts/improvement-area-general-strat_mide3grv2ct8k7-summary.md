  - Opening (5-10 minutes): Recap and player intent gathering

    - DC 16 success rates for challenging tasks
    - Implications for gameplay reliability
  - **Vulnerability Patterns** with mitigation strategies

- Added "How Attribute Distribution Shapes Gameplay" summary section with 5 key principles:
  1. High Attributes (2) define reliable competencies (75-90% success)
  2. Middle Attributes (1) provide flexibility but not mastery (45-60% success)
  3. Low Attributes (0) create interesting constraints (35-55% success)
  4. Skills and Edge shift probabilities by 10-20%
  5. Different archetypes create different gameplay experiences

**Content Added:** ~280 lines
**Estimated Impact:** Addresses reviewer requests for guidance on how attribute choices affect gameplay. Provides concrete probability data to help players understand the mechanical impact of their choices. Shows how different builds create distinct play experiences.
